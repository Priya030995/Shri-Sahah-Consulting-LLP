About Us

Shri Sahah Consulting LLP is an accounting firm based out of Nanganallur (Chennai) which redefines accounting & compliance services and believes in “Quality meets trust.”
It was incorporated on October 2022. The term “Sahah” means “Mighty partner” and thus we are a strong team with experience in varied domains to fast-track our clients' business by allowing them to focus on their core, by managing their end-to-end accounting & compliance requirements efficiently and effectively.
